# Quality, standards, and risk

**Parent:** [`README.md`](../README.md)

## What this probes

Raising or defending **quality under pressure**: testing, reviews, safe environments, release risk, “stop the line.” Overlaps ethics prompts—answer with **process and risk judgment**, not a morality play, unless you have a real proportional story.

## Example questions

| # | Question | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Tell me about making quality non-optional (gates, reviews, CI). | Load testing gate. |
| 2 | Pressure to skip tests or cut corners—how did you respond? | Escalation + evidence. |
| 3 | Tell me about improving CI or release quality. | SDK ecosystem / pipelines. |
| 4 | How do you test when the real environment is unavailable? | Safe mock / sim environment. |
| 5 | Walk me through risk assessment before a risky release. | Dependencies, rollback. |
| 6 | When did you stop or delay a release? | Courage + process. |
| 7 | How do you define “done” for engineering quality? | Test bar, observability. |
| 8 | Tell me about catching a serious bug before production. | Detection mechanism. |
| 9 | Security or privacy consideration you championed. | Boundary if none. |
| 10 | How do you balance review thoroughness and velocity? | Team norm story. |
| 11 | Tell me about improving observability to catch issues earlier. | Metrics / alarms. |
| 12 | Describe an ethical dilemma or pressure to ship something unsafe. | Often **process** answer; see [sensitive-prompts](sensitive-prompts.md). |
