# Story recovery prompts

Template fields for each prompt:
- Company/year
- Stakes
- Counterparty
- Constraint
- Metric
- What I would do differently

## Ownership
1. What was your hardest project at Amazon?
2. When did you work without clear requirements?
3. When did you improve something nobody asked for?
4. Which system became your de-facto responsibility?

## Conflict
5. When did you disagree with architecture direction?
6. When did priorities conflict across teams?
7. When did you need to convince more senior engineers?

## Failure
8. Biggest production issue you were involved in?
9. A time your estimate was wrong?
10. A migration or rollout that went wrong?
11. A communication mistake that created delay?

## Performance / scale
12. Biggest latency reduction you contributed to?
13. Biggest reliability improvement?
14. Biggest throughput improvement?
15. Biggest operational cost reduction?

## Ambiguity
16. Undefined requirements you had to shape?
17. Legacy system with poor documentation?
18. Cross-team dependency chaos example?

## Prioritization
19. Deadline conflict where you had to cut scope?
20. Tech debt vs feature pressure decision?
21. A time you said no and why?

## Mentorship
22. Onboarding someone under time pressure?
23. Review feedback that changed team quality?
24. Teaching a concept that unblocked delivery?
