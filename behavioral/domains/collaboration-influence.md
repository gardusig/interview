# Collaboration and influence

**Parent:** [`README.md`](../README.md)

## What this probes

Work through **others** without authority: peers, PMs, partner teams, slow stakeholders. Signal is **unblocking**, crisp communication, and shared outcomes—not “I convinced them because I was right.”

## Example questions

| # | Question | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Tell me about working with a team that had different priorities. | Catalog / cross-team. |
| 2 | How do you unblock work when you do not own the other team’s roadmap? | Options + escalation. |
| 3 | Tell me about a slow stakeholder or partner. | **Behavior**, not “toxic.” |
| 4 | When did you drive consensus across teams? | Concrete artifact or meeting outcome. |
| 5 | Describe influencing technical direction without being the tech lead. | SDK ecosystem fits. |
| 6 | Tell me about “managing up” or keeping leadership informed. | Short, factual. |
| 7 | When did documentation reduce repeat questions or friction? | Onboarding / SDK docs. |
| 8 | Cross-functional project—what was explicitly **your** role? | Avoid “we” soup. |
| 9 | When did you run or facilitate a meeting to move work forward? | If thin, boundary OK. |
| 10 | How do you give credit vs take credit fairly? | One example. |
| 11 | Tell me about aligning engineering and product on a controversial item. | Data + user impact. |
| 12 | When did you have to follow a decision you disagreed with? | Commit-after-decision maturity. |
