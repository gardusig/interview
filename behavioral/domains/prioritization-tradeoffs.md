# Prioritization and tradeoffs

**Parent:** [`README.md`](../README.md)

## What this probes

Can you **choose** under finite time: features vs debt, evidence vs hope, scope vs date? Good IC answers show **how you decided**, what data or principles you used, and how you **communicated** tradeoffs-not only that you were busy.

## Example questions

| # | Question | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | How do you decide what to work on first? | Tie to team goals + risk. |
| 2 | Tell me about choosing tech debt vs a new feature. | Who agreed? |
| 3 | When did you cut scope to meet a deadline? | What was cut, what was protected. |
| 4 | Describe negotiating timeline with a PM or stakeholders. | Stay respectful of PM. |
| 5 | Tell me about a time you had too much WIP; what did you do? | Concrete WIP limit or sequencing. |
| 6 | When did data change your priority order? | Metrics, experiment, incident. |
| 7 | How do you say no to work requests? | “No” vs “not now” vs escalate. |
| 8 | Tell me about deprioritizing your own idea because something else mattered more. | Maturity signal. |
| 9 | When did you push back on a roadmap? | Pair with collaboration framing. |
| 10 | How do you balance perfection and shipping? | Definition of good enough. |
| 11 | Tell me about a time you had to choose between two urgent requests. | Clear decision criteria. |
| 12 | How do you handle recurring interrupts vs deep work? | Process story OK if real. |

## Gaps and detail drills

- **Drill:** for load-testing and profile stories, rehearse **what exactly was deprioritized**, **who signed off**, and **what evidence** you showed (graphs, error budgets, etc.).
